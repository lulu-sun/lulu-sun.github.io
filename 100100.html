Dodo's Day

A short and sweet RPG for a best friend's wedding 4 June 2022

Dodo's Day is a 2D pixel story game loosely based off Pokemon. In this game, the main character (the bride!) loses her memories, and has to earn them back through trials (minigames) set by her bridesmaids. At the end of the game, she finds her "heart's desire" (spoiler alert!), and unlocks a sentimental photo album (with contributions from the bridesmaids!) as well as some congratulatory messages from those who couldn't make it.

The game was made in Unity over the course of two months. We spent (almost) every hour of our free time not only building the world of Dodo's Day in C#, but also sourcing and creating artwork and music, compiling creative ideas from bridesmaids (and making them happen!), and tracking down every missed collision tile.
